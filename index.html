UX Meetup in Jozi
Saturday, 25th January, 2013

Local is lekker, but so is some international experience.

South African, Rob Enslin, spent quite a few years practising UX in the UK. He has now moved back to South Africa! Before he knew he was back permamently Rob took it upon himself to start a UX meetup in Braamfontein, Johannesburg. Rob pulls from his vast knowledge and experience in UX and his work for ThoughtWorks in the Agile UX and consulting space.

Attending his meetups have been an enlightening experience where I have managed to get some much needed hands on practise with user reasearch and protyping. Although he has only hosted 2 meetups so far, they have been regular and provide an extremely fresh approach to meetups in general.

Mobile UX Rob's second meetup, held on the 12th of January was all about Mobile UX. The meetup started with a presentation and then moved into hands on mode where everyone got to try their hand crafting user research questions, asking those questions and then presenting back to the group. Although this sounds like a lot of hard work, Rob's fascilitation approach means that even those who aren't necessarily UX professionals felt comfotable and had fun.

Some comments from Meetup.com

Manny Ferreira - Really awesome, well structured learned a lot in small amount of time, enjoyed it immensely.
Siphiwe Gwebu - Great meetup guys! Being a non-UX person, I was worried that it might be alienating but I was pleasantly surprised to find that it was informative and engaging. Well done!

Welcome back Rob :)
